About Us

We are based in Manchester, offering remote or house visit tutoring for any student in Year 4 to Year 13. Whether you are struggling with school work or just looking for some extra help with homework we are available for most subjects.

5 Star Tutoring in Manchester

Welcome to Book Star Tutoring

We aim to get to know the student before offering any tutoring. All our tutors aim to understand the needs of each students before designing the best methods to help the student understand what they are being taught.

We understand that everyone learns differently and having a personalied plan is important to learning. Before we allocate a tutor and start planning the best methods of learning to students, we ask both parents and students to fill out a questionnaire each to understand what both sides are looking for.

Tutoring from Home

Are tutoring is available from three places, remote tutoring, tutoring from the tutors house or tutoring from the students house. We understand that there is many different situations for students, to be flexible we arange locations with students and parents a day before the date for tutoring. We aim to make sure that students are comfortable with any decision and they ahve the nessacary resources for the tutoring session.

Since March 2020, we have introduced the option of remote tutoring, as we all stayed at home we knew it was still important for students to have their education and help when needed. During this time we made sure that student and parents knew that all our tutors were available.

Our history

We were established in 2010. Our aim is to help students enjoy learning and help them find methods to learn which is both fun but also productive.

2010

March 2010, Book Star started when our owner saw a young member of her family find it difficult to learn and wasn't able to understand why education is important. So she decided to start Book Star and help young people enjoy learning and understand the importance.

2012

During June 2012, as we started growing with more and more people getting to know what we offer we decided that there is more than one methods for young people to start to learn. This is when we decided to make the path of learning different for each student.

2014

When we first started we were only helping students from year 4 to year 6 with any homework or unsure work they were taught at school. In early 2014 we decided it was time to expand and help students in the KS3 as well.

2016

After adding KS3 into our service we found that many students in secondary education was finding it very difficult to learn at school and was looking for more support outside of school so we decided to expand again and work with KS4 students too.

2018

As we continued to look for ways to grow we found that saying goodbye to our students was hard after they finished their GCSE. We had some students asking us whether we would consider expanding more and tutor KS5 Students. With consideration we decided that helping students is our main goal and seeing there was a market to tutor KS5 we decided to start this service in September 2018.

2020

2020, was a difficult time for many students having to learn from home and finding it difficult to catch up, we saw many students stuggl and knew the only method to help is to continue our service but provide it through Zoom. We made sure that all our students had the resources to learn during the lockdowns in the UK and made sure that they knew all our staff were available during any time within the day.
